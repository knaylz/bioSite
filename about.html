About

Melanie is an active grandmother who loves jogging, walking and cycling during warm months. She’s lived an exciting life as a horse trainer in the late 1970s and early 1980s, but now her life is quite the opposite, although admittedly just as exciting. She now focuses all of her time on what she says matters most: family. She enjoys shopping and lunches with her two daughters. Her two grandchildren also keep her busy, and there is nothing better than taking them both for the day. You can usually find her at the beach, a restaurant, listening to music like Cher or Lady Gaga or spending time in her garden. She’s most known for her tomato plants, and this year she is trying to have 42 plants so that she can make some of her favorite tomato dishes including tomato soup, pasta sauce and french fish soup. Her goal is to produce enough veggies to make sizeable donations to local shelters. Everything she grows is organic, so handing it off to those who could really use would make her so happy. She’s most proud of a recent hobby she’s picked up, which is remodeling her home. So far there is one major project under her belt, the kitchen. However, that’s not where this story ends; she’s got big plans for the living room. The plans include knocking down a wall and install hardwood over carpet; these are two things she’s been anxious to do for many years. She couldn’t be happier with her life and hobbies. Now it’s time to share them with you!
